## The Skill

This skill outlines a systematic methodology for inventing project, app, and portfolio concepts that fit my unique profile: a creative, product-minded individual based in Melbourne with a variety of hobbies and a diverse career background. The goal is to generate ideas that are distinct, buildable, and high in portfolio value, avoiding generic SaaS or startup boilerplate.

### 1. Core Invention Principles
* **Personal Relevance & Buildability:** Combine my interests (LEGO, music, gaming, coding, DJing, art, poetry) with realistic solo build constraints.
* **Specific Tools over Vague Platforms:** Prioritize tight, functional utilities (e.g., setlist energy mapper) rather than large, complex networks or marketplaces.
* **Obvious Demo Moment:** Ensure each idea has a clear "show me" user workflow (e.g., input file/text -> receive visual or audio output) demoable in under 60 seconds.
* **Small, Complete, Polished:** Focus on a single excellent workflow, strong visual identity, and minimal backend (Vercel serverless friendly, no accounts required).
* **Deliberate AI Use:** Choose the build mode (No AI, Optional AI, AI-assisted, or AI-core) based on whether it is genuinely justified.
* **Privacy & Safety First:** Prefer local-first client-side data processing. Avoid storing sensitive identity data or building surveillance-like tools.
* **Authentic Voice:** Present ideas in a dry, practical, observant, and slightly odd tone, strictly avoiding corporate hype words.

### 2. Intersection Formula
Generate unique project ideas by combining:
* **One Interest:** e.g., LEGO, DJing, Poetry, Writing, Photography.
* **One Work Context:** Retail, Advertising, Insurance, Ecommerce, Accounting.
* **One Playful Mechanic:** Draggable blocks, game loops, visual fossils.
* **One Build Constraint:** Seeded client-side generator, Web Audio API, Canvas.

### 3. Preferred Output Patterns
* **Creative Input &rarr; Structured Output:** e.g., lyrics to melody, DJ setlist to energy curve, photo to colour palette.
* **Data &rarr; Playful Object:** Turning API usage, weather, or plans into physical/visual metaphors.
* **Constraint-Based Generators:** Restricting options (e.g., 5-note song generator) to trigger creativity.
* **Strange Interfaces for Practical Tools:** Prioritizing tools (e.g., product priority game) with memorable, custom interactions.

## Challenges

- **Avoiding Cliché & Hype**: Ensuring concepts bypass generic B2B SaaS tropes and remain aligned with my practical, product-minded, yet slightly quirky design taste.
- **Scope Calibration**: Designing highly compelling and visual demos while maintaining a strictly scoped MVP that a single developer or AI coding agent can build in a few days.
- **Coherent Intersections**: Crafting natural intersections between very different domains (e.g., LEGO and cryptography, or retail and game mechanics) without producing overly complex or confusing user flows.
