## The Skill

This skill provides comprehensive guidelines for assisting with online shopping in Australia. It draws on extensive purchase history and strong category preferences to identify genuine deals, filter out fake discounts, and stick to trusted brands and platforms.

### 1. Core Shopping Profile

The skill defines detailed purchase behaviours and deal thresholds for high-confidence categories:
* **3D Printing & Fabrication:** Filament (PLA, PETG, TPU), printer parts (Bambu Lab, Creality), and maker tools.
* **Tech Accessories:** USB-C/Thunderbolt cables, power banks, storage enclosures, and docks (preferring UGREEN, Anker).
* **Gaming & VR:** Handheld accessories, Meta Quest gear, and experimental interaction hardware.
* **Computing & Networking:** Raspberry Pi, mini PCs, SSDs, and mesh Wi-Fi gear.
* **Audio & Studio:** MIDI controllers, audio interfaces, and IEMs.
* **Creative & Collectibles:** Camera accessories, LEGO (Botanicals, Icons, Ideas), tabletop gaming storage, and art supplies.
* **Practical Consumables:** Pet supplies (cat food/litter), household staples (cleaning, batteries), snacks, and tools (Bosch, Wera, iFixit).

### 2. Deal Filtering Rules

* **Fake Discounts:** Always verify against historical pricing (CamelCamelCamel, OzBargain) and ignore fake RRPs.
* **Shared Account Exclusions:** Filter out categories that clearly belong to shared account members (e.g. feminine-coded clothing/products) unless they match a known interest.
* **Generic Brands:** Avoid obscure marketplace brands for safety-critical or electronic items. Accept them only for simple, low-risk accessories (e.g. cable clips).
* **International Stores:** Only consider if the landed price is highly competitive, delivery times are reasonable, and there is low warranty/safety risk.

### 3. Price Sensitivity

* **Small items ($5-$20):** Surface if useful or repeatable.
* **Mid-tier accessories ($20-$60):** Surface if aligned with a strong category and genuinely discounted.
* **High-ticket ($150+):** Requires very strong alignment and verifiable price drops.

---

## Challenges

- **Fake RRPs**: Retailers often inflate the base price to make a standard sale look like a massive discount. 
- **Counterfeit Goods**: High risk for storage media, batteries, and safety equipment on marketplaces.
- **Shared Account Noise**: Distinguishing the target user's preferences from those of the other person sharing the account.

---

## Future Improvements

- **Automated Price Tracking**: Integrating direct hooks into CamelCamelCamel or OzBargain APIs to programmatically verify if a price is at an all-time low.
- **Shipping Calculators**: Better handling of total landed cost when dealing with international Amazon listings or niche hobby stores.
